Welcome to NatureDex

NatureDex is your AI-powered wildlife exploration companion. Designed for hikers and nature lovers, our app allows you to identify animals on the go using just your smartphone camera. Stay safe with real-time alerts and learn more about the animals you encounter in the wild.

The Problem

Exploring nature is rewarding, but it comes with the challenge of recognizing wildlife, especially dangerous species. Hikers often encounter animals like snakes, bears, or other wildlife without proper knowledge of their behavior or how to react, leading to potential risks.

The Solution: NatureDex

NatureDex uses AI to identify animals in real time, providing instant information about the species, its habitat, and whether it poses any threat. Our safety alerts warn you of dangerous animals in your vicinity, helping you plan safe hiking routes and avoid unnecessary risks.
